How to Verify
  1. Scan the QR code on the certificate using any QR scanner app
  2. Copy the decoded text (it will be a JSON string)
  3. Paste it in the box above, or use the Certificate ID
  4. The system verifies the RSA-2048 digital signature
  5. A green result means the certificate is authentic
  6. A red result means the certificate is forged or tampered
